About Me!

@Engineering Student

With a passion for coding and problem-solving, Chrisha has honed skills in programming languages such as Python and Java and has a strong interest in creating UI designs. She also participated in an entrepreneurship program under the small company Lakapati, supported by the Junior Achievement of the Philippines (JA Philippines).
